A Decade of Song Covers

A knowledge graph searching engine for over 30,000 cover songs in time period from 2012 to 2022.

Overview

A Decade of Song Covers contains information about cover songs and the related information like original song and performers. It aims to help people explore music. Data resources include Secondhandsongs, Last.fm, and wikidata. There are over 30,000 songs in the database.
